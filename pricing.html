Common questions

What People Ask First

How do I know if I'm a fit for the 90-Day Turnaround Sprint?

The Sprint is designed for organizations that are functionally insolvent, stagnant, or structurally broken — not simply slow-growing. If your organization has exhausted conventional options, has leadership willing to make hard decisions, and has a mission worth saving, you are likely a fit. The discovery call is specifically designed to answer this question honestly.

Can individuals who aren't business owners benefit from the consulting?

Absolutely. The Gift Discovery Intensive and the Mastermind Group are specifically designed for individuals — including employees, ministry workers, creatives, and professionals — who want to identify and monetize their God-given gifts, whether through a side business, a career pivot, or a new ministry initiative.

Do you work with clients outside Connecticut and New England?

Yes. The majority of active engagements are conducted virtually or via a combination of virtual and in-person sessions. Pastor Anderson has worked with organizations and individuals across the United States and has engaged with leaders internationally, including in West Africa.

Is faith a requirement to work with FaithVision Leadership?

No. While faith is the foundation of Pastor Anderson's personal practice and worldview, the strategic frameworks and results they produce are available to any leader or organization willing to engage seriously with the process. The track record speaks for itself regardless of the client's faith background.

How quickly can an engagement begin?

Discovery calls are typically available within 3–5 business days of inquiry. Engagements can begin within 1–2 weeks of completing the discovery process and confirming scope. Availability is limited — Pastor Anderson maintains a small active client roster to ensure the quality of attention each engagement receives.

The First Conversation Is Free

Every engagement begins with an honest 20-minute discovery call. No sales pressure. Just a direct conversation about where you are, where you want to go, and whether FaithVision Leadership is the right fit to help you get there.
